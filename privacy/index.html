Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Card Tag is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Photo Library Access

Card Tag requests access to your photo library only to:

  • Let you choose photos to add labels to
  • Save your exported (labeled) images back to your device

Photos are used only on your device. We never upload, store, or transmit your images.

No Data Sent to Servers

All processing happens on your device. Photos stay on your iPhone or iPad. We do not send your images, labels, or any data to our servers or any third party.

No Accounts Required

Card Tag does not require sign-up or login. We do not collect personal data such as names, emails, or account information.

No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use analytics, advertising, or any form of tracking. The app does not collect usage data or identify users.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at contact@cardtag.ie.
